Evan Williams Distillery

Located in Louisville's famed and historic "Whisky Row", Evan Williams is unique that the product is distilled in one location and bottled in another - both in Kentucky, of course. First introducted in 1957, Evan Williams bourbon is ranked as one of the world's top selling bourbons.

Address: 528 West Main Street, Louisville, KY

Open Thursday through Saturday 11AM-5PM and Sunday 1-5PM
