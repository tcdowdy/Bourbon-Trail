Woodford Reserve Distillery

Located in Woodford County, west of Versailles, Kentucky, Woodford Reserve Distillery is a National Historic Landmark. Located on the same site since 1812, Wordford Reserve's Kentucky Straight Bourbon Whiskey is known for it's aromatics, spice, fruit and floral flavors - all in the same bottle!

7785 McCracken Pike, Versailles, KY

Open 9AM-5PM Wednesday-Saturday and 12-4:30PM on Sunday
