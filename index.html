Kentucky's Bourbon Trail

Main Tour Passport Distilleries

Kentucky's Bourbon Trail is a program sponsored by the Kentucky Distiller's Asscociation. The program began in 2008 with seven distilleries and has grown to 18 on the Main Tour and an additional 20 artisan distilleries on the Craft Tour. Visit them individually, or map out your own tour.

This map provides a glance at the Main Bourbon Trail distilleries located along the Kentucky Bourbon Trail. - Note: Craft distilleries appear in the map as smaller icons but are not included in the list below.
